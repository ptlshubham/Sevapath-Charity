About Us / અમારા વિષે

The Seva Path Story

Seva Path Charitable Trust is a non-profit organization working since 2009 to empower tribal and rural communities through healthcare, education, skill training, and animal welfare.

સેવા પથ ચેરિટેબલ ટ્રસ્ટ ૨૦૦૯થી આરોગ્ય, શિક્ષણ, કુશળતા વિકાસ અને પ્રાણી સેવા દ્વારા આદિવાસી અને ગ્રામ્ય સમાજને સશક્ત બનાવવા માટે કાર્યરત છે.

Our mission is to bring sustainable growth and essential services to the heartlands of Gujarat, ensuring that no one is left behind in the journey towards progress and dignity.

It’s about empowering people and communities to grow with confidence. For years, we’ve provided essential support in health, education, and animal welfare, tailored to the unique needs of tribal regions. Our dedicated team combines passion with action, helping individuals navigate challenges and seize opportunities for a better tomorrow.

Community-First Mindset

We prioritize the needs of tribal and rural communities, delivering localized solutions with integrity and compassion.

Sustainable Impact

We focus on long-term growth through education and vocational training, enabling sustainable livelihoods for the youth.

Compassionate Service

Our service extends to all living beings, including animals, promoting kindness and care across the ecosystem.

Compassion. Service. Empowerment.

Community empowerment is at the heart of everything we do — for our beneficiaries, our team, and the region. We are dedicated to helping tribal families reach new levels of self-reliance through health camps, skill development, and cultural preservation initiatives.

By understanding the local challenges, we deliver solutions that foster sustainable progress and long-term value. Together, we grow stronger — today and for the future.

2009: Founded with a vision to bring light and hope to the tribal regions of Kawant, Seva Path started as a small volunteer group dedicated to community service.

Our early focus was on primary health awareness and emergency support for rural families.

2012: Expanded our reach with formal registrations and the launch of organized health camps across the district.

2015: Introduced skill development programs for tribal youth, focusing on employable skills like nursing assistance and craftsmanship.

2020: Pivoted to large-scale relief and medical support during challenging times, strengthening our community bonds.

Present: Today, Seva Path is a multi-faceted NGO with dedicated wings for Health, Education, Skill, and Animal Welfare, serving thousands every year.
