Frequently Asked Questions

How can I donate?

You can donate via Bank Transfer, UPI, or through our online portal. Every contribution is 80G tax-exempt.

Is Seva Path 80G registered?

Yes, we are a registered charitable trust and provide tax-exempt receipts for all donations under section 80G.

How can I volunteer?

You can sign up through our Volunteer page or contact us directly. We have both on-ground and remote opportunities.
