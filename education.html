Our Approach

Our education initiatives are designed to bridge the gap between rural potential and modern opportunities. We focus on market-aligned skills that ensure immediate employability and long-term financial independence for tribal youth.

We believe that education is not just about literacy, but about enabling dignity and self-reliance through mastery of skills.

Placement Success 85% Employment
What We Offer

Course Modules

Nursing Assistant Training

Specialized healthcare training for tribal girls, including hospital internships and certification for professional nursing roles.

Diamond Polishing

Vocational training in diamond artistry, connecting local youth with Gujarat's world-renowned diamond industry.

Basic Computer Literacy

Empowering students with digital skills, from office tools to internet safety, essential for any modern job role.

Soft Skills & Communication

Training in interview skills, personality development, and spoken language to prepare students for the corporate world.

Why Join Our Academy

Our Skill Academy doesn't just teach — it transforms. We offer a 100% free learning environment, industrial exposure, and placement support that has changed the lives of thousands of tribal families across Gujarat.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is eligible for these free courses?

Youth from tribal and rural regions of Gujarat are eligible. We prioritize those from underprivileged backgrounds who are seeking vocational skills for employment.

Is there any fee or hidden cost?

No, all our training programs, including material and practical sessions, are 100% free for the students.

Do you provide job placement support?

Yes, we have a dedicated placement wing that connects our certified students with hospitals, diamond units, and other industrial partners.

What is the duration of the courses?

Most of our vocational courses range from 3 to 6 months, designed for quick skill acquisition and employment.
